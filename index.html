— Our Story —

BUILT BY
BROTHERS

Repliclassics was founded by brothers Brian and Gary Jones out of Bowling Green, Kentucky. Gary brings a lifetime of expertise in fabrication and paint. Brian handles the business. Together they built a shop that doesn't cut corners.

Their focus is the 1967–1972 Chevrolet truck and Blazer — a specific era they know inside out. From the custom tubs they fabricate for 1969–72 Blazers to the full frame-off restorations, every vehicle gets the same treatment.
